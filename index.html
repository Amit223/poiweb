Milan is Italy’s city of the future, a fast-paced metropolis where money talks, creativity is big business and looking good is an art form. In Paris, choosing a dress is a monumental decision. In Milan, it’s a kick .Milan is a true metropolis: strong and fearless but welcoming, too. Little by little, I came to realize that I could become someone here. In Milan, everything is regulated by money. They say “cappuccio” in bars instead of “cappuccino” to save a syllable.
